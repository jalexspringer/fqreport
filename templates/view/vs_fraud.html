Viewability vs. Fraud

Initially when botnets first began being used to generate fraud they were relatively unsophisticated and used simple browsing models such as rapid-refreshing pages to maximize fraudulent impressions generated. As bots have advanced in sophistication they have also started to fake advertiser KPIs such as:

  • Viewability – in some cases fooling even MRC accredited vendors
  • Engagement – mouse movement, clicks, scrolling
  • Exploiting cookie history/retargeting
  • Conversions – accomplished through using stolen credit cards to fake purchases
  • Filling out forms
