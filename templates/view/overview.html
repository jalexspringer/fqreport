Forensiq defines Viewability per the MRC standard of 50% of the ad in-view for 1 continuous second for display inventory and 2 continuous seconds for video inventory. Forensiq offers two products as part of its Viewability offering:

  • Viewability Detection: measures whether 50% of the ad is in-view continuously at the following intervals:
    • Display: 1s, 5s, 10s, 30s
    • Video: 2s, 5s, 10s
  • Hidden Ad Detection: ads hidden from view by having been placed behind other ads, in a background browser window or positioned in a way in which the user could not have seen the ad.
